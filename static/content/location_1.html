Pub ambience of drinks clinking, men chattering, beer being poured into glass steins.

The year is 1828 and two strangers have just immigrated to this dark and dingy city. They're using what money they have to enjoy a pint at the pub where they meet for the first time.

The doors burst open, BURKE saunters in defeated, drops onto a bar stool, slams money onto a wooden counter. The pub is fairly quiet apart from a few regulars, HARE and two STUDENTS conversing over their course at the local Medical School.

BURKE... Pint please.

BAR MAID... Yes sir.

The BAR MAID rushes off, pours a pint, places it down in front of Burke on the wooden counter and takes the money gingerly.

HARE... Rough day?

Burke takes a glug of his pint and slams it back down, wiping froth from his moustache.

BURKE... You wouldn't know the half of it.

HARE... Think I would. Got a Tennant that hasn't been paying his rent lately. Barely paid for my pint I'm struggling that much because of him.

BURKE... Some people are unreal. What's your name?

HARE... Hare. You?

BURKE... I'm Burke. What you drinking Hare? Next one's on me.

HARE... I'll take a whiskey please.

BURKE... Sure.

Burke hits the bar twice to get the Bar Maid's attention.

BAR MAID... Can I help you sir?

Burke slams money onto the wooden counter.

BURKE... Get this one a whiskey.

BAR MAID... Certainly.

The Bar Maid rushes off, pours a whiskey and places it in front of Hare. She then takes the money quietly and rushes off. Hare picks up the whiskey and turns to face Burke.

BURKE... Cheers!

They both drink.
